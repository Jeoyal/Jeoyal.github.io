About

Hello, I am a third-year Computer Science Ph.D. student at Tongji University, luckily advised by Prof. Cairong Zhao. Prior to this, I obtained my bachelor degree in Intelligence Science and Technology from NJUST in 2022. At present, I am doing research intern in OpenMMLab of Shanghai AI Laboratory, Shanghai, supervised by Dr. Yanan Sun. I also have a deep academic collaboration with Dr. Xinyang Jiang from Microsoft Research Asia. My research interests are in image/video generation, privacy & security and person Re-ID, etc.
